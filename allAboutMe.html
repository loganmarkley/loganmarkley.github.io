All About Me!

Hello! My name is Logan Markley.
I am a junior studying towards a Bachelor's in Computer Science.
I attend the Missouri University of Science and Technology, and I plan to graduate in May of 2025.

I currently work as a Peer Learning Assistant for the S&T Learning Enhancement Across Disciplines (LEAD) program on campus.
I facilitate small-group tutoring sessions and collaborate with two other PLAâ€™s to assist students with coursework and topics in Engineering Physics I.
I focus on inspiring learning without giving out solutions and rather through asking questions and making sure that students truly understand the how and why.
Around 35 students attend these sessions which occur twice a week, and I love helping my peers comprehend and apply what they learn in lecture!

I also work on-campus as a Student Ambassador for the S&T Computer Science Department.
The main focus of the role revolves around giving prospective families an overview of the Computer Science program at Missouri S&T.
I do this by having a sit-down discussion with them which allows for a comprehensive talk about courses, extracurriculars, and more.
After the discussion, I take them on a guided tour of the CS building, noting important features such as the professors' offices and popular rooms.
And, of course, many questions are asked along the way, and I do my best to answer them and expand upon them to make the families feel comfortable and welcome!
